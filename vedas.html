Introduction

The Vedic texts are the oldest foundations of Hinduism. The word "veda" means "to see" or "know". The Rigveda was among the first texts, and was adapted from oral storytelling. The Baghavad Gita, "Song of God", is an epic based upon the Vedic scriptures, and originates from around 3rd-5th century BCE.
